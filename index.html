About the event
NEXTGEN Research 2026

NEXTGEN Research 2026 was an undergraduate research workshop organized by the Green Club of the Faculty of Computing & Technology, University of Kelaniya, held on 5th July 2026. The workshop was designed to introduce undergraduates to the world of research — covering what research is, how to conduct it, how to think critically, and the opportunities it opens. Participants heard from researchers and academics working at the frontiers of their fields, and were equipped with practical knowledge to begin their own research journeys.
